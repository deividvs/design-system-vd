Circular badge that holds a single line icon — used in feature/tool cards and list rows.

```jsx
<IconBadge tone="forest">
  <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="22" height="22" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><path d="M3 3v18h18"/><path d="m19 9-5 5-4-4-3 3"/></svg>
</IconBadge>
```

- `tone`: `forest` (default) · `amber` · `solid` (forest gradient, white icon) · `danger` · `dark`.
- `size` is the diameter (default 48). Icons inherit `currentColor`.
