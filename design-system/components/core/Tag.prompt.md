Uppercase eyebrow / pill label — sits above headlines or marks a section. 11px, wide tracking.

```jsx
<Tag tone="light">Feito para pecuaristas</Tag>
<Tag tone="amber">Oferta de lançamento</Tag>
<Tag tone="dark">Gestão · Decisão · Lucro</Tag>
```

- `tone`: `light` (forest on cream, default) · `amber` (money) · `dark` (use on forest-950 grounds) · `danger`.
- `iconLeft` accepts a small icon node.
